Collision Repair In Polokwane

From fender-bender to structural rebuild, we put accident damage right.

Request a Quote WhatsApp Us

After an accident, the damage you can see is only half the story. Our collision repairs start with a structural assessment: measuring the body against manufacturer datum points to find every bend and shift before work begins. That assessment becomes a detailed, insurance-ready quotation you and your assessor can trust.

Repair then proceeds in sequence: structure straightened, panels repaired or replaced, seams welded and sealed, and the vehicle refinished in our spray booth. Nothing is handed back until it passes our quality control against the original specification.

What's Included

  • Structural assessment against manufacturer datum points
  • Chassis check and straightening where required
  • Panel repair and replacement
  • Weld, seal and corrosion protection
  • Insurance-ready quotation and claim support

Typical Jobs

  • Front-End Rebuild
  • Side-Impact Repair
  • Quarter Panel Replace
  • Accident Assessment

Why It Matters

Structural accuracy is a safety issue: crumple zones, airbag sensors and suspension geometry all depend on the body sitting exactly where the manufacturer intended. We repair to that standard, not to 'close enough'.
