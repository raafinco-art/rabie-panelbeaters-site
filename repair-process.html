Our 8-Step Repair Process

The same disciplined sequence on every vehicle, whether it's a scratch or a rebuild.

  1. 01

    Inspection

    We examine the damage in person or from your WhatsApp photos: what's visible, what's likely hiding behind it, and what it means for safety.

  2. 02

    Assessment

    Structure, panels and paint are measured against factory specification so the full scope of work is known before any metal moves.

  3. 03

    Quotation

    You receive a detailed, insurance-ready quote. The price we give is the price we hold; customers have trusted our estimates for years.

  4. 04

    Repair

    Structural straightening, panel beating and replacement happen first: the metalwork that decides whether a repair lasts.

  5. 05

    Preparation

    Repaired surfaces are skimmed, sanded, primed and blocked flat. Paint only ever goes over preparation we would sign our name to.

  6. 06

    Painting

    Your exact colour code is matched by computer and applied in our dust-controlled booth: primer, base and clear, blended into the neighbouring panels.

  7. 07

    Quality Control

    Panel gaps, paint depth and finish are checked against the original specification. Anything short of factory standard goes back.

  8. 08

    Delivery

    The vehicle is detailed, and we walk the repair with you at collection so you can see exactly what was done.

What To Bring To Your Assessment

  • Your vehicle, or clear photos of the damage via WhatsApp
  • Insurance claim number, if you are claiming
  • Vehicle licence disc details
  • Any previous repair history you know about
