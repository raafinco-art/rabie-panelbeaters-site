Built On Straight Panels And Straight Answers

Rabie Panelbeaters repairs accident damage, dents and paintwork for drivers across Polokwane from our workshop at 12 Mangaan Street in Nirvana. Panel beating is exacting work: metal must be reshaped to the millimetre, gaps aligned, and paint matched so the repair disappears.

That discipline extends to how we quote. On Google, one reviewer summed it up as being "probably the best when it comes to panel beating cars"; on Facebook, every customer who has left a review recommends us. That reputation is worth more to us than any single job.

From the first inspection to the final polish, your vehicle is handled by one team in one workshop, and we treat every car like our own.

What We Stand For

Precision

Factory specification is the only standard we accept, on structure, panel gaps and paint.

Trust

Quotes that hold. Timelines we communicate. No surprises when you collect your keys.

Craftsmanship

Panel beating is a trade. Our technicians treat every panel as proof of their skill.

The Workshop

Our facility in Nirvana is equipped for the full repair chain, so nothing is outsourced and nothing is rushed:

  • Dust-controlled spray booth with computerised colour matching
  • Chassis and frame alignment equipment
  • Panel fabrication and welding bays
  • Dedicated preparation and finishing areas

The people behind the work are qualified panel beaters and spray painters who have made this trade their career. When you drop off your vehicle, you speak directly to the team doing the repair.
