Privacy Policy

Effective 15 July 2026

The Short Version

This website does not collect, store or track your personal information. When you send an enquiry, the message is composed in your browser and opens in your own WhatsApp or email application. You send it yourself, and you keep a copy.

What This Website Does Not Do

  • No cookies are set
  • No analytics or tracking scripts run
  • No form data is stored on any server
  • No information is shared with third parties

What We Receive When You Contact Us

If you send an enquiry via WhatsApp, email or phone, we receive whatever you include in that message: typically your name, contact details, vehicle information and a description of the damage. We use it solely to assess, quote and carry out your repair, in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

How Long We Keep It

Enquiry messages stay in our WhatsApp or email history so we can reference your quote and repair. We do not add you to marketing lists.

Your Rights

Under POPIA you may ask what personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email rabiepanelbeaterssa@gmail.com and we will respond promptly.

Third-Party Services

The contact page embeds a Google Map, and sending via WhatsApp uses WhatsApp's service. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies once you interact with them.
