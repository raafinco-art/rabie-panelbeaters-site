Paint & Finish

Yes. We match your exact factory colour code with computerised colour matching, then blend the new paint into the neighbouring panels so there is no visible edge, including metallics and pearls.

A single panel is typically a few days including preparation and curing. Full resprays and structural repairs take longer; you get a realistic timeline with your quote, and we tell you immediately if anything changes it.

Hand-wash only for the first month, no wax or polish for 60 to 90 days, and park out of harsh sun where you can while the finish fully cures. We run through this with you at collection.

Workmanship & Guarantees

We stand behind our workmanship. Ask us about the guarantee on your specific repair when you book; the terms depend on the type of work being done.

Body and paint repairs done to specification with proper materials are designed to preserve your vehicle's warranties. Raise any specific warranty conditions with us at assessment and we will work within them.
